How to Play?

Guess the Footballer in six tries.


Each guess must be a current Premier League footballer, with a 2nd name being 5 letters. Hit enter to submit.


After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to indicate how close your guess was.

Example:

S A L A H

The letter 'S' is in the correct position.

Y O U N G

The letter 'N' is in the word, but a different position.

P O G B A

The letter 'O' is not in the word.

Enjoy a new Footballdle every day!
